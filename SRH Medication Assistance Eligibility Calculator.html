Program settings · income limit and poverty figures

The pharmacy medication assistance program qualifies households at or below 500% of the federal poverty level. There is no sliding scale, so eligibility is a single income limit. Poverty figures are the HHS 2026 guidelines for the 48 contiguous states and DC. Update the two dollar fields each January when new guidelines publish.

This is a screening estimate, not a final determination. It compares household income to 500% of the federal poverty level, the pharmacy's income limit for medication assistance. Final eligibility is determined by SRH Specialty Pharmacy using verified income documentation. A household over the limit may still qualify on a case by case basis under the financial assistance policy.
